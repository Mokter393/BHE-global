About BHE Global

The UK’s most trusted University representative was established in 2012. Boost Education Service has 10 years of operational experience in the UK education industry. In addition, We have highly educated and experienced admission advisers, counsellors, compliance officers, lawyers, and marketing experts to provide excellent admission support for potential applicants. Most of our employees hold a UK University master’s degree. They have vast knowledge of the UK education system, admission process, entry requirements, and education market intelligence. Our employees are trained by UK universities, UCAS, British Council, UK NARIC & English UK and other educational institutions. Boost Education Service has recruited over 4000 International, EU & Home students in the UK since 2012. Over the world, fifty (50) countries students applied to UK Universities through the Boost Education service.
